// 02 · The Solution

One Tool. Everything
the ER Doctor Needs.

BrainBuddy sits silently beside the EMR — watching what the doctor types, delivering the differential, tests, drugs and billing codes before the doctor even asks.

Real-time symptom detection
6,096 SNOMED CT US synonyms. 171 diagnoses. 225+ typo & shorthand corrections. Detects what the doctor types — abbreviations, colloquialisms and all. Powered by SNOMED CT US Edition (382,170 concepts).
Precision diagnosis engine
Context-aware scoring with profile modifiers. Panic vs ACS. GERD vs MI. 22 context profiles suppress false positives before they reach the doctor.
Recommended tests
STAT / Urgent / Conditional triage. Auto-populated from detected symptoms. Tap any test to copy — paste directly into EMR orders section.
First-line drug reference
51 condition profiles. Exact dosing. Brand and generic. Tap to copy into EMR. No internet lookup. No switching apps. Everything is offline.
ICD-10 + CPT billing codes
Auto-suggested from top diagnoses. Paste-ready for any billing system. No manual lookup. No missed codes. Immediate revenue recovery from day one.
One-tap H&P note generation
Full structured SOAP note built from the session. Copy to clipboard — paste directly into Epic, Cerner, or any EMR free-text field. Done.
// 03 · How It Works

The Doctor Never
Leaves the EMR

BrainBuddy runs in parallel — always on top, never in the way.

1
Doctor opens EMR
BrainBuddy auto-positions to the top-right corner of the screen. Silent. Out of the way. Ready.
2
Doctor scribes symptoms
Doctor types normally in the EMR. BrainBuddy captures every word in real time — the doctor never switches apps.
3
Sections fill instantly
Word by word: Symptoms → Diagnoses → Tests → Drugs → Codes. All 5 sections populate simultaneously.
4
Doctor taps to copy
One tap per item or per section. Paste into any EMR field. Each item flashes green on copy — instant confirmation.
5
Generate H&P Note
One button. Full SOAP note to clipboard. Paste and done. 5–10 minutes saved per encounter.
